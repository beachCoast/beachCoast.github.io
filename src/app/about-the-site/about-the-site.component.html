About The Site

More than just about Cars

Celebrating Dorset

I will be showing you some of the fantastic places to go here in Dorset for food, drink and adventure. I will be introducing you to local businesses and entrepreneurs. There are many great drives in and around Dorset, I intend to take you on these drives and show you some of the beautiful sights.

Car Blogs

The type of car blogs you will see will vary from talking about classic, modern and new cars. I will be writing blogs from Q and A's from actual car owners, about what is the best car for a budget. I want to write blogs that make you laugh and that you can relate to.

Car Life.. Car Passions

I will be bringing you blogs of all things car related.. such as car shows, books, people, car clubs just to name a few. There are so many topics in this area and I want to bring them to you.
